About Me

Hi, I'm Torin Dove.

I'm a Finance and Economics student with a minor in Financial Planning, and I created Torin Dove Financial to share what I find genuinely interesting about markets, money, and investing — in a way that's actually digestible and entertaining.

That might mean breaking down a major market story happening right now, doing a deep dive on an individual stock, or exploring a concept that's been sitting in my head. Whatever it is, I cover it the way I'd want it explained — using real terms, real analysis, and clear context. No dumbing it down, but no gatekeeping either.

Outside of content creation, I'm a Research Fellow at CAPE Research Corp, where I work on political finance data and investment research. I'm also President of my school's Investment Club and Finance Society, and VP of the Financial Planning Club — and I competed as a CFA Research Challenge Local Champion.
